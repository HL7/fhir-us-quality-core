Quality applications may make use of patient-specific information. For this reason, all transactions must be appropriately secured, limiting access to authorized individuals and protecting data while in transit (as laid out in the [FHIR Implementer's Safety Check List]({{site.data.fhir.path}}safety.html#7.10.1)). These considerations relate to any FHIR implementation, including authentication, authorization, access control consistent with patient consent, transaction logging, and adherence to best practices.

The [US Core Security]({{site.data.fhir.ver.uscore}}/security.html) section provides additional expectations that apply to this guide.
